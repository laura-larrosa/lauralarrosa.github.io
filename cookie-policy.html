Cookie Policy

Effective date: on July 19th 2023


Introduction

Welcome to our website. We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to gather information about how you interact with our site. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.


What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your device (computer or mobile device) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over a certain period, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.


How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for the sole purpose of collecting information through Google Analytics. Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google that tracks and reports website traffic, allowing us to better understand how users interact with our site. The information collected by Google Analytics cookies is anonymous and does not personally identify you. We use this data to analyze trends, improve our website, and optimize your browsing experience.


Your cookie choices

By default, most web browsers automatically accept cookies. However, you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and some features may not work as intended.


Third-party cookies

We do not use any third-party cookies apart from Google Analytics on our website.


Updates to our Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our data practices. When we do, we will revise the "effective date" at the top of this page.
