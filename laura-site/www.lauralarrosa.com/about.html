A bit about myself

Hi there! I'm Laura, a Product Designer with over 4 years of experience crafting digital products, plus 5 years in the world of industrial and graphic design (yep, I’ve been through it all!). My passion is designing experiences that make people’s lives easier, always keeping one eye on the details... and the other on usability!

Right now, I’m lucky to be part of Proqio, where we design solutions that literally help to keep tunnels from collapsing and bridges from falling down. As a team, we've managed to innovate in the infrastructure sector, reducing risks and saving a few engineers and builders from some serious headaches.

I take a practical and hands-on approach to everything I do. I love working in a team, and as cliché as it sounds, I firmly believe that the best ideas can come from any corner of the office. I’ve led projects with multidisciplinary teams, always looking for that perfect balance between what the user needs and what the business wants. And when it comes to challenges, I don't shy away, I treat them like puzzles just waiting for a good design solution!
